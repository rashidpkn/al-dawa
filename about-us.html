Pharmacy on myAster

AL DAWA GROUP OF PHARMACY's flagship application, the myAster app, allows its users to access AL DAWA GROUP OF PHARMACY online. Now users can explore and order from a wide range of health, wellness, fitness, lifestyle, homecare, and medical products across hundreds of brands.
